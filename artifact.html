Aside from the espresso machine at the heart of the cafe, Caffe Reggio displays a number of historical artifacts including over 80 pieces of art. The dramatic works add to the unique antique environment of the cafe that has kept customers returning in the midst of the Starbucks and Dunkin Donuts around the city.

A historical bench that was once owned by the Medici family from the 15th Century.

A 16th century painting by an artist who was a student of the famous Italian painter Michelangelo Caravaggio.
